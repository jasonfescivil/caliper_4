# Tekoa WWTP — I&I and System History (2006 → Present)

----

### 2006–2010  
**I&I & River Influence Study:**  
* Hangman Creek stage ↔ sewer flows  
* Floodplain/high groundwater drives infiltration  
* Seasonal pattern: late winter/spring  

----

### 2017 Monitoring (5-min at D1, B7N, A25, F1N):  
* Sharp RDII pulses; D1/B7N surcharge  
* Weirs maxed; AV velocity methods  
* 4 basins ~69% of I/I (~45% ERUs)  
* Spring WWTP flows > design  

----

### 2018 Targeted Improvements:  
* Relocate mains from trashed lids  
* Rehab brick MHs near seasonal streams  
* Focus on D-line / Crosby–Ramsey  

----

### 2019–2021 DMR:  
* No daily >0.9 MGD  
* AADF ≈ 0.11–0.12 MGD  
* Seasonal I&I without extremes  

----

### 2020–2021 Lift Station DMR:  
* Design PHF ≈ ADWF × PF(3) + I&I → ~404 gpm  
* Abandon 10" siphon (likely major I&I)  
* New 12" C900 inlet; Triplex VFD  
* Two pumps ≈ 650 gpm total  

----

### 2022–2024 DMR:  
* Emergence of daily spikes > 0.9 MGD  
* Align with rainfall; consistent with 2017 RDII behavior  
* Suggests persistent hotspot basins & storm clusters  

----

### 2024–2025 Characterization:  
* Flat AADF (OFM-based)  
* RDII top events ranked  
* Synthetic hourly hydrographs  
* EQ envelope for Q_allow 180–240 gpm  

----
