**RE:** National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) Permit No. WA0023141  
Noncompliance Notification  

Dear Mayor Jaeger:  

Ecology reviewed the City of Tekoa’s compliance with NPDES Permit No. WA0023141, issued on June 1, 2014. The City of Tekoa has numerous missing permit required submittals and discharge monitoring report violations (DMR). The enclosure provides a summary list of the DMR violations discussed in this warning letter.  

According to our records, Ecology needs Tekoa to resolve the the following issues:  

1. **Missing permit submittals**  
* Annual Wasteload Assessment due July 1, 2014 and July 1, 2015  
* Annual Operations and Maintenance Manual Update or Review Confirmation Letter due July 1, 2014 and annually thereafter  
* Industrial User Survey due March 1, 2015  

**DMR reporting errors and violations**  
Tekoa appears to have more violations that are actually occurring due to errors using the permit required web based (WebDMR) online reporting system. From June 2014 through January 1, 2016 they reported approximately 177 violations. Tekoa had three late DMRs and 27 numeric effluent violations or exceedances of design criteria (see enclosure). The remaining violations resulted from reporting code errors, typing errors, and/or failing to take a sample.  

Ecology provides technical assistance to help communities learn how to use WebDMR, Ecology’s web based reporting system. We have resources in the Eastern Regional Office available to your staff to help prevent future reporting errors. Additionally, we can provide an Excel spreadsheet that allows your staff to copy and paste directly from the spreadsheet into WebDMR.  

Our region’s wastewater operator technical assistance provider, Andy O’Niell can work with your operator on how and when to take samples so that the facility does not miss samples needed to stay in compliance.
