# spokane-concurrence-3-25-2010.pdf

March 25, 2010

USDA  
Janice Roderick  
Environmental Coordinator  

**Re: Teko Washington, Sewer & Water System**  

Ms. Roderick:  

Thank you for contacting our Spokane Tribe of Indians is much appreciated.  

Pursuant to compliance with Section 106 of the National Historic Preservation Act we are continuing consultation for this project.  

We concur with your recommendations of the APE; we believe it is important to assure that the prior work efforts are incorporated into the final survey report to provide a definitive understanding of the site with definable boundaries.  

Thank you for the cultural survey report, and the Spokane tribe would like to monitor this project this area is a high concern to the tribe, we also meet secretary interior standards and specialize in our own culture.  

However, if any artifacts or human remains are found upon inadvertent discovery, this office should be notified immediately and the work in the area cease.  

Should additional information become available our assessment may be revised.  

And consider this a positive action that will assist us in protecting our shared heritage.  

Lem Lmts,  

Randy Abrahamson  
Tribal Historic Preservation Officer.
