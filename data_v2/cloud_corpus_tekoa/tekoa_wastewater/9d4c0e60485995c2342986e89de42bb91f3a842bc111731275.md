# FINDING OF CATEGORICAL EXEMPTION (SEPA)

It is the Finding of The City of Tekoa, WA that the following activities are Categorically Exempt under the Washington State Environmental Policy Act (SEPA) (RCW 43.21C.110 and WAC 197.11.800).

(NOTE: If project is Categorically Exempt under SEPA, the SEPA Checklist is not required.)

## Location of Activities:

* _The project area is located within The City of Tekoa, including portions of Section 13 and Section 24, Township 20 North, Range 45 East and Section 19, Township 20 North, Range 46 East. It includes areas in the project description below._

## Description of Activities:

* _The project will replace approximately 3,900 linear feet of sanitary sewer pipe and associated manholes that are experiencing extremely high amounts of infiltration and inflow. Sewer lines to be replaced include those along East Main Street and Lindsey Street, North Crosby Street and Poplar Street, and along the ballpark from Warren Street to Seamon Road._

## Reasons for Categorical Exemption under SEPA:

**WAC 197-11-800 (3):**

> “The following activities shall be categorically exempt: The repair, remodeling, maintenance, or minor alteration of existing private or public structures, facilities or equipment, including utilities, recreation, and transportation facilities involving no material expansions or changes in use beyond that previously existing.”
