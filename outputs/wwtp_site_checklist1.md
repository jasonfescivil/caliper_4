assistant: Purpose: This checklist helps evaluate candidate WWTP sites in Washington for technical feasibility, environmental protection, regulatory consistency, and constructability. It aligns with Ecology’s Criteria for Sewage Works Design (Orange Book) and key Washington submittal requirements for general sewer plans, engineering reports, and plans/specs, including receiving water, flood, I/I, outfall, reclaimed water, sludge, costs, staffing, and SEPA/NEPA statements [8; WAC 173-240-050/060/070: [2] p.4; [4] p.6; [1] p.7].
