assistant: Purpose: This checklist helps Washington project teams screen and document the suitability of a proposed wastewater treatment plant (WWTP) site against Ecology’s Criteria for Sewage Works Design (Orange Book) and required submittals under WAC 173-240, with cross-references to reclaimed water (WAC 173-219), receiving water quality (WAC 173-201A), and related permits. Use it to assemble the engineering report content, evaluate constraints (flood, shoreline, groundwater, wetlands), and plan reliable access, utilities, and construction phasing.
