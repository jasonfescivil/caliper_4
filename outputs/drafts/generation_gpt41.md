Below is a master outline for a small-community (<1000) wastewater treatment feasibility report in Washington. Each section maps to the appropriate requirements in WAC 173-240, the Criteria for Sewage Works Design ("Orange Book"), and relevant federal references. Purpose statements and citation anchors are provided for each section.

---

# MASTER OUTLINE – Small Community Wastewater Treatment Feasibility Report

---

## 1. Executive Summary
- **Purpose:** Briefly summarizes findings, recommendations, and key data for decision-makers.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060, Orange Book G1-4.1.1 [7], [10]

---

## 2. Introduction and Purpose of Project
- **Purpose:** Define the need for the project, project drivers (regulatory compliance, public health, environmental protection).
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3)(b); Orange Book G1-4.1.1, G1-3.2(1), G3 [7], [10]

---

## 3. Project Organization, Ownership, and Service Area
- **Purpose:** Identify owner/operator, authorized reps, description of service area, and organizational structure.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3)(a), (b); Orange Book G1-3.2(2)-(3) [7], [10]

---

## 4. Regulatory Framework and Permitting
- **Purpose:** Identify and summarize applicable federal, state, local, and tribal regulations governing wastewater projects.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3); Orange Book G1-1.3 to G1-1.6, G3-1.1, G1-2.1 [1], [2], [8], [10]

---

## 5. Existing Conditions
- **Purpose:** Describe current facilities, system maps, collection system status, and known problems.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3)(c), (j); Orange Book G1-3.2(4), G1-4.1.1; EPA "Design Criteria for Mechanical, Electrical, and Fluid System Component Reliability" [7], [10], [1]

---

## 6. Wastewater Characterization and Load Projections
- **Purpose:** Detail present and future flow/loading (quantity/quality), including I/I and industrial or commercial contributors, and 20-year projections.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3)(c), (j); Orange Book Table G1-1, G3-1.1.3; EPA [7], [1], [10]

---

## 7. Receiving Environment/Discharge and Disposal Analysis
- **Purpose:** Describe receiving water or land, applicable water quality standards, dilution/mixing zones, and groundwater protection.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3)(e), (l); Orange Book G1-3.2(4), Table G1-1, G1-4.1.1; EPA [3], [2], [10]

---

## 8. Identification and Evaluation of Alternatives
- **Purpose:** Systematic review of feasible alternatives (technologies, sites, nonstructural options, no-action), including environmental and cost impacts.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3)(f); Orange Book Table G1-1, G3-2.1, G1-4.1.1; EPA alternatives analysis [3], [7], [1]

---

## 9. Selected Alternative Preliminary Design Basis
- **Purpose:** Develop preliminary design of the preferred alternative, including process description, site plan, hydraulic profile, and preliminary sizing.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3)(f), (i); Orange Book Table G1-1, G1-4.1.1; EPA [3], [7], [10], [1]

---

## 10. Environmental and Cultural Resource Review
- **Purpose:** SEPA review, environmental impacts, potential for historic/cultural impacts, compliance with federal NEPA if federally funded.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3), SEPA (Ch. 173-802 WAC), NEPA, SERP; Orange Book G1-2.7, G1-4.1.1, G3-1.1.7 [10], [2], [5], [8]

---

## 11. Infiltration/Inflow, Overflows, and System Vulnerabilities
- **Purpose:** Assessment and management of I/I, bypasses, SSOs, risk of natural hazards.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3)(j); Orange Book Table G1-1, G1-3.2 [5], [7], [10]

---

## 12. Industrial and Non-Domestic Waste Considerations
- **Purpose:** Address industrial waste treatment requirements, source identification, and pretreatment needs.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3)(k); Orange Book Table G1-1; EPA pretreatment standards [7], [1]

---

## 13. Outfall and Disposal System Analysis
- **Purpose:** Engineering analysis of proposed outfall/diffuser or land disposal system, modeling of plume and compliance zones.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3)(l); Orange Book Table G1-1, EPA "Technical Support Document for Water Quality-based Toxics Control" [3], [7]

---

## 14. Financial Plan and Project Funding
- **Purpose:** Project financing options, user/connection rate analysis, funding eligibility (SRF, grants), capital and O&M cost estimates.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3); Orange Book G3-2.2; EPA/Washington SRF Guidance [1], [10]

---

## 15. Project Implementation Plan and Schedule
- **Purpose:** Include milestones for design, permitting, funding, construction, and startup of the proposed facilities.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(2); Orange Book G1-4.1.1, G1-3.2 [7], [10]

---

## 16. Appendices
- Maps, flow diagrams, hydraulic analyses, sampling locations, soils/hydrogeological data, SEPA/NEPA documentation, public participation records, supporting calculations.
- **Purpose:** Provide backup documentation for report findings as required by reviewers.
- **Citations:** WAC 173-240-060(3); Orange Book Table G1-1, G3, US/EPA [3], [1], [10]

---
