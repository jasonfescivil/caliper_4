assistant: The G1 requirements for engineering reports are outlined in Table G1-1 [1,2] of the "DOE Orange Book Design Standards" [1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8] and WAC 173-240-060 [8]. 

Engineering reports must include detailed, project-specific evaluations of alternatives [1,3] considered to implement projects described in a general sewer plan [1,3,7]. They must be sufficiently complete [1,2,3,8] and contain appropriate preliminary design details [1,3,8] so that any engineer can develop complete design documents [1,3] (plans and specifications) without making substantial changes. [1,2,3,8] 

Reports must establish the rationale for specific design criteria [1,3] and include a description of the project [8], treatment process [8], basic design data and sizing calculations [8], site recommendations [8], and relevant data as requested by Ecology. [8] 

Three copies of the report [8] must be submitted for approval [8], except when waived. [8]
