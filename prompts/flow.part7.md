5.4.3	Minimum Flow
Calculating daily minimum flows is necessary to ensure that self-cleansing velocities are maintained within the gravity portions of the sewer collection system, preventing the deposition of solids and subsequent blockages, odor issues, and corrosion. It is also important for the proper operation of pumps (to avoid excessive short-cycling which can damage equipment) and some treatment processes that may be sensitive to very low flow conditions or require a minimum hydraulic throughput.
