# Prompt 2 — From Checklist → “Consequences & Added Scope” Matrix (Washington-specific)

Using the WWTP Site Consideration Checklist you just produced, create a “Consequences & Added Scope” matrix that maps common checklist outcomes to design constraints, additional studies, eliminated options, and permitting paths in Washington.

Deliverables (in this order):

1. A brief usage note (2–3 sentences) stating that this matrix is applied after the checklist is filled for a specific site.
2. A single Markdown table with these columns:

- Checklist Outcome / Condition
- Design/Permitting Consequences
- Additional Scope Triggered
- Alternatives Affected
- Cost/Schedule Impact (Low/Med/High; include typical schedule drivers)
- References (WAC/RCW/Federal/Local)

Concrete mapping examples to include:

- CARA (High susceptibility): Infiltration/percolation for reclaimed water ruled out or requires liners + GW monitoring + setbacks; hydrogeologic assessment (WAC 173-200; RCW 90.46).
- FEMA Floodway: site generally not suitable; if 100-yr floodplain, elevate floors above BFE, floodproof critical equipment, verify access during floods (FEMA NFIP; WAC 173-240-060(3)(h)).
- Shoreline jurisdiction: Shoreline conditional use permit, mitigation sequencing, no-net-loss (RCW 90.58).
- Wetlands present: delineation, buffers, mitigation plan; likely no unlined ponds/infiltration (CAO; 404/401).
- Receiving water on 303(d)/TMDL: anticipate more stringent limits (e.g., temperature/ammonia); require receiving-water & mixing-zone analysis (WAC 173-201A; TMDL).
- Steep/unstable slopes/liquefaction: geotech exploration, ground improvement/piles, slope stabilization.
- Sensitive receptors nearby: odor covers/scrubbing, noise barriers, larger buffers; outreach plan.
- Insufficient power: utility coordination; larger standby generator; long-lead equipment risk.
- Long force mains/multiple lift stations: higher O&M; surge analysis; EQ at pump stations; energy impacts.

Style: concise and actionable; Washington-specific; cite standards inline in References. Return only the matrix (no extra narrative) in copy-pasteable Markdown.
